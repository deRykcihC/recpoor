**Privacy Policy**

**Last updated:** February 10, 2026

**1. Introduction**
recpoor ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application "recpoor" (the "App") collects, uses, and discloses information.

**2. Data Collection and Usage**

**a. No Personal Data Collected**
The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data, including names, email addresses, or phone numbers. We do not use any third-party analytics services or advertising networks that track user behavior.

**b. Screen Recordings and Audio**
The App's primary function is to record your device's screen and internal audio.
*   **Local Storage**: All recordings are saved locally on your device in the public "Downloads" directory.
*   **No Uploads**: We do not upload your recordings to any server. Your content remains completely private on your device unless you manually choose to share it.

**c. Crash Logs**
The App may collect anonymous crash logs through the Google Play Console to help us identify and fix stability issues. These logs do not contain personal information.

**3. Permissions**

To function correctly, the App requires the following permissions:
*   **Microphone (RECORD_AUDIO)**: Required to capture internal audio (on Android 10+). We do not record your voice or ambient validation unless explicitly stated.
*   **Storage (WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE)**: Required to save recorded video files to your device's storage.
*   **Foreground Service (FOREGROUND_SERVICE)**: Required to keep the recording service active while you use other apps.
*   **Media Projection**: Required to capture the screen content.

**4. Third-Party Services**
The App has no affiliation with third-party tracking or advertising services. It operates entirely offline.
