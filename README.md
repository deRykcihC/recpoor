# recpoor

**recpoor** is a lightweight, ad-free screen recorder designed for performance and simplicity. It captures high-quality video with internal audio support on compatible devices (Android 10+), offering a pure recording experience without the bloatware found in many other apps.

## Key Features

- **Pure Screen Recording**: No watermarks, no ads, and no time limits.
- **Internal Audio Support**: Capture crystal-clear device audio directly.
- **High Quality**: customizable bitrate selection up to 16 Mbps.
- **Quick Settings Tile**: Start and stop recording instantly from your notification shade.
- **Minimalist Design**: A clean interface that respects your system's "Material You" theming.
- **No Bloatware**: Designed strictly for recording, with no unnecessary features or background services draining your battery.

## Security & Privacy

- **Offline Operation**: This app works completely without an internet connection.
- **Local Storage**: Recorded files are saved directly to your **Downloads** folder. Your recordings stay on your device and are never uploaded to any server.

## Requirements

- **Internal Audio Requirement**: Android 10 (Q) or higher
